Our Vision.

The team of Sanjeevani Pearl Humanity is focusing to provide education to children who cannot afford it due to unfortunate condition of their family. Our vision is to educate them so that they can fulfil all their basic needs. We feel education is the right weapon they should hold to make a change.

About

Check out the stuff that we do


We look for children who are helpless. We provide primary education by ourself. Later, we enroll them in schools for better education. The fund collected is used to pay the school tuition fee, rest amount is kept as saving for their further studies.

We have good quality teachers from different institutes of Physics, Chemistry, Biology and Mathematics. This will ensure a better guided path to choose their career.


Contribute to Sanjeevani Pearl Humanity
